Little‑Known Tax Hacks

Claim an 80TTA deduction (₹10k) on savings‑account interest—most people forget.
Bought health insurance for parents? Section 80D lets you deduct an extra ₹25k‑₹50k.
Renting and no HRA? Use Section 80GG to still claim rent up to ₹60k per year.
Interest on education loans is fully deductible under Section 80E for 8 years.
Use ELSS mutual funds: 3‑year lock‑in + lowest 80C lock period + potential equity gains.
